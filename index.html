About BrightSmile Dental

Founded in 2008, BrightSmile Dental has been providing exceptional dental care to the Johannesburg community for over 15 years. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology to ensure comfortable, effective treatments.

We believe in a patient-centered approach, taking the time to understand your needs and concerns. Our team stays current with continuing education to offer you the most advanced treatments available.

  • Modern, sterile facility
  • Pain-free treatments
  • Flexible payment options
  • Emergency appointments available
